Get from A to B and make a friends along the way

Senior Ride Nashville is an affordable, assisted ride service that pairs a senior who needs a ride with a volunteer who can give one. Learn more by watching the video below.

"Senior Ride Nashville made the transition to my new ‘car-less life’ so very easy for me. I appreciate the quality of the volunteer drivers. They are without exception outstanding people. Thank you." — Senior Ride Nashville rider
“My volunteer driver was one of the best people I’ve met in a long, long time. This was far better than having a neighbor take me to the doctor. I didn’t really know what to expect for my first ride, but this was just great.” — Senior Ride Nashville rider
“Since I cannot drive, I lost some of my independence. Senior Ride has made it possible for me to regain my independence with transportation in going to doctors, therapy, and errands. The drivers are always dependable and friendly. Senior Ride has been a blessing for me and I am very thankful for this service.” — Senior Ride Nashville rider
