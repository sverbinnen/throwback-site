Give a Gift

Volunteering to drive is one way to give a gift, donating is another. Your gift gives older adults the ability to get around town and foster relationships with members of our community, which makes the city we live in better for everyone.

OPTION 1: DONATE ONLINE BY CLICKING BELOW

OPTION 2: MAIL OR CALL IN A DONATION

Senior Ride Nashville
298 Foster Street
Nashville, TN 37207

(615) 610-4040

Please make your check out to Senior Ride Nashville.

OPTION 3: GIVE BACK WITH KROGER COMMUNITY REWARDS

Select Senior Ride Nashville to be the beneficiary of your Kroger Community Rewards. Every time you shop with your rewards card, Kroger will donate a portion of its profits without costing you anything or taking away any of your rewards points. Click the Kroger logo to login to your Kroger Community Rewards.* Search for Senior Ride Nashville (or our code: KB561) under Find an Organization and click enroll.

*If you haven’t logged in with Kroger before, it will prompt you to create an account to link to your new or existing Kroger Rewards Card.

OPTION 4: MAKE A DONATION THROUGH AMAZON

You can support Senior Ride Nashville each time you make a purchase using Amazon Smile. Click the Amazon logo to select Senior Ride Nashville as the organization you support - and ask your friends to do the same!

OPTION 5: DONATE A VEHICLE THROUGH DONATE FOR CHARITY

Have a car you no longer want? Running or not, Donate for Charity can pick it up free of charge. Call Donate for Charity at 866-392-4483 or click the logo to go to Senior Ride Nashville’s donation form. We thank you, in advance, for choosing Senior Ride Nashville to receive the proceeds of your donation.
