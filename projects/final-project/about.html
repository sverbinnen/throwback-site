About Me

Hey, I'm Ella! I am a digital, surrealist artist based out of Providence, Rhode Island. I have been creating art my entire life but began making physical collages in early 2016. Later that year, I found my love for digital collages and the freedom they provide me as a creative outlet.

My commissions are always open! In the past, I have worked on album artwork for local bands and individual commission requests. I have also vended my work at multiples shows and art crawls in Providence. Please feel free to contact me at any time via my contact information below or on my Contact page.
