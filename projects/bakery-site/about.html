Meet Our Team

A great guest experience requires the efforts of many people.
At Vittoria’s we are lucky to have a restaurant filled with team members that have what we like to call ‘the Hospitality Gene.’
Our staff takes pride in creating great experiences for all of our guests and their passion continues to inspire us.

With our store managers Michael Lacz and Marco Rizzo leading the way our team at Vittoria’s has built a reputation for great food,
fast service and even better conversation. Any team is only as strong as the people that are apart of it and we are fortunate to
have a fantastic bunch behind the counter at Vittoria’s!
